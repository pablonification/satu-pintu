## Presentation Tips

### Do's:
1. **Start with the problem** - make it relatable
2. **Live demo is CRUCIAL** - this is a tech hackathon
3. **Show the AI in action** - judges love seeing tech work
4. **Keep slides minimal** - max 5-6 bullet points per slide
5. **End with clear CTA** - what do you want from judges?

### Don'ts:
1. Don't read slides verbatim
2. Don't spend too long on tech details
3. Don't skip the demo (it's your strongest asset)
4. Don't forget to mention the "tracking" aspect - it's the key differentiator

### Timing Guide (7 min total):
| Section | Time |
|---------|------|
| Problem | 1 min |
| Solution | 1 min |
| Demo | 2 min |
| Tech & Differentiators | 1 min |
| Market & Roadmap | 1 min |
| Team & Ask | 1 min |

### Backup Plans:
1. **Demo fails?** Have a video recording ready
2. **Internet issues?** Have local screenshots/GIFs
3. **Time running out?** Skip market/roadmap, keep demo + ask
